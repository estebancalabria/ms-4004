# 🇺🇸 English

## 📊 Plan Categories Overview

Microsoft 365 (formerly Office 365) offers plans across 6 categories:

| Category | Best For | Users |
|---|---|---|
| 🏠 **Personal & Family** | Individuals and households | 1–6 people |
| 💼 **Business** | Small & medium businesses | Up to 300 users |
| 🏢 **Enterprise** | Large organizations | Unlimited users |
| 🎓 **Education** | Students and faculty | Educational institutions |
| 🏛️ **Government** | US government agencies | Government entities |
| 🦺 **Frontline** | Field/factory workers | Deskless workers |
